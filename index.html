How we evaluate

Here is the two most inmportant thing for a JEE aspirants to care proper care of if he wants to clear the exam. We have this feature which helps our users to prepare for the exam.

Marking Scheme

For each incorrect answer, 1/4th mark of the total mark allotted will be deducted. Each question carries 4 marks. But don't worry our test papers are designed so well that you will understand exactly the problem and how to tackle it. SO start to solve the test papers before wasting much time now. I will more than happy if you will clear the exam.

Time Management

It usually happens during the final exam that students fail to complete the test paper. Why? The real reason being their inability to manage time properly. Despite of being thoroughly prepared for the exam, candidates tend to feel tensed and nervous on the exam day. We will help you in tackling this issue.
